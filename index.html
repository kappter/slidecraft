Welcome to SlideCraft

SlideCraft is designed for students and educators to create interactive process guides. Follow these steps:

  • Select a theme (Light or Dark) for best visibility.
  • Upload a CSV file with steps (Step, Description, Order Number, Image URL, Duration).
  • Check 'Auto-Advance' to move slides automatically based on duration (default 5 seconds).
  • Click 'Start' to begin the guided process.
  • Complete the quiz and generate a PDF report.

Our vision: Empower learning with simple, mobile-friendly tools for process mastery.
